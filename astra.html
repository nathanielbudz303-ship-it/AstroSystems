360Â° Airfoil Body

The entire airframe acts as a wing, ensuring nearly 100% of the surface area contributes to lift. This distinctive design eliminates dead weight and creates a "Total-Body Lift" effect that drastically reduces drag and power consumption, allowing for mission durations that far exceed standard multi-rotor capabilities.

Gimbaled VTOL Propulsion

Unlike fixed-rotor systems, Astra's motors tilt 90 degrees to transition from vertical takeoff to forward flight. This vectoring capability directs high-speed airflow across the back of the airframe, artificially boosting lift and keeping the drone airborne with minimal energy expenditure.

Helical Swarm Ascent

For heavy payloads, three drones link together and spiral upward in a synchronized helical path. This unique ascent strategy distributes the weight evenly and generates immediate aerodynamic lift from the airfoil bodies upon takeoff, significantly reducing the vertical load on individual motors.
